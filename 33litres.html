33 Litres was born in 2021 through a lot of thoughts as the place where everything around is an art project.

In 2024, it transformed into what we called the 3s: "the Studio" for our private exploration and development within the realm of all forms of architecture, "the Society" for creative nomads, those who feel that everything around them is an art project too, and "the Sessions" just to have some extra-fun.
