LUCÍA CARVAJAL

is a fashion student in Burgos. Between her various passions in life we can see photography and fashion scratching the surface

Her work presented here is a hybrid of both artistic areas. In her next photographs we can perceive an avant-gard touch. We welcome you with her session #1

“NEGATIVE PHOTO”

Her session #2 reflects the quirkiness and an exaggerated version of her raw vision of youth. Always with a “chicness” incorporation.

“QUE YO NO ME VOY A CASAR”

And lastly, her session #3 inspired in retro Vivienne Westwood titled:

“NOT REAL VIVIENNE”

shows us the power of retouching, filters and photographic post-production as in representing the visual identity of a brand.
